About Big Mac Index


Since the big mac is considered to be identical among the countries due to its kind of basic ingredients, analyzing the big mac index provides quick and simple insight about the purchasing power of the country. Comparing the data of prices, currency rates, and GDP, the economic parameters can be interpreted together and help to make decisions.


People no longer need to be economic experts to analyze the purchasing power while making their travel plans. As a web application, our app would like to provide them quick insight, so that people can evaluate the monetary and economic situation among the countries easily. To present this information, our app gathers the data and shows it on suitable graphs to present the information all together.
